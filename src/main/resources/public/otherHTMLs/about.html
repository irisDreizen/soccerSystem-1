Company History

Far far away, in the middle of the desert, there is a group of students who dreamed about a lot of free time, spending time in the free pool of Ben Gurion university, partying on Wednesday-Baraka and eat Humus on friday. But then they found out that they have a huge project of soccer system to work on. so they left all their dreams and made as much as they can to bring this system for you. now, when it's ready to use - you can find us in Humus-Abu-Dubby.
